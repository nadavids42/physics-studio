# Introductory Mechanics — curriculum plan

**Status: unimplemented planning outline. No code backs this document.** It lists the
proposed lesson sequence and titles only. Five lessons currently exist as real,
learner-completable content (`m01-measurement-models`, `m02-position-velocity`,
`m03-motion-graphs`, `m05-constant-acceleration`, and the projectile-motion-from-components
lesson); everything below beyond those is not implemented and should not be presented as
active course coverage.

## Unit 1 — Measurement, models, vectors, and graphs

- `m01-measurement-models` — Measurement, units, uncertainty, and models
- `m02-vectors-coordinates` — Coordinates, scalars, and vectors
- `m03-motion-graphs` — Reading motion graphs

## Unit 2 — One-dimensional motion

- `m04-average-instantaneous` — Average and instantaneous motion
- `m05-constant-acceleration` — Constant-acceleration models
- `m06-free-fall-review` — Free fall and one-dimensional synthesis

## Unit 3 — Two-dimensional and projectile motion

- `m07-vector-kinematics` — Two-dimensional kinematics
- `m08-projectile-components` — Projectile motion from components
- `m09-projectile-applications` — Projectile applications and cumulative kinematics review

## Unit 4 — Forces and free-body diagrams

- `m10-interactions-newton` — Interactions and Newton's laws
- `m11-free-body-diagrams` — Free-body diagrams and force models
- `m12-friction-inclines` — Friction and inclined planes

## Unit 5 — Work and energy

- `m13-work-kinetic-energy` — Work and the kinetic-energy theorem
- `m14-potential-conservation` — Potential energy and conservation
- `m15-dissipation-energy-review` — Dissipation, power, and cumulative energy review

## Unit 6 — Momentum and collisions

- `m16-impulse-momentum` — Impulse and momentum
- `m17-collision-models` — Elastic and inelastic collisions
- `m18-momentum-synthesis` — Collision synthesis and cumulative review

## Unit 7 — Rotation, torque, and center of mass

- `m19-angular-kinematics` — Angular kinematics and rotational inertia
- `m20-torque-equilibrium` — Torque, levers, and static equilibrium
- `m21-center-mass-rotation-review` — Center of mass and rotational synthesis

## Unit 8 — Gravitation, orbital motion, and course synthesis

- `m22-universal-gravitation` — Universal gravitation and gravitational energy
- `m23-orbits` — Circular, elliptical, and escape trajectories
- `m24-mechanics-synthesis` — Introductory Mechanics cumulative synthesis

Note: the shipped lesson IDs (`m01-measurement-models`, `m02-position-velocity`,
`m03-motion-graphs`, `m05-constant-acceleration`, projectile-motion-from-components) do not
map one-to-one onto this outline's numbering — `m02-position-velocity` is not the same
lesson as `m02-vectors-coordinates` above. Reconciling the two is unimplemented work.
